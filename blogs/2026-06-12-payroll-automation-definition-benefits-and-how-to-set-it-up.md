Running payroll is one of the most critical responsibilities for any business, but it’s also one of the most time-consuming. From calculating salaries and deductions to filing taxes and issuing pay stubs, even small errors can lead to compliance risks, unhappy employees, and wasted hours for payroll teams. For small and mid-size businesses, where resources are often stretched, managing payroll manually can quickly become overwhelming.
