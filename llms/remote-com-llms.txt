- [Create a company](https://developer.remote.com/reference/post_v1_companies.md): Creates a new company.    ### Creating a company with only the required request body parameters   When you call this endpoint and omit all the optional parameters in the request body,   the following resources get created upon a successful response:   * A new company with status `pending`.   * A company owner for the new company with status `initiated`.
